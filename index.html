Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What makes calibrated plywood better?

Calibrated plywood is double-pressed under strict thickness tolerances (usually ±0.2mm). This provides a perfectly flat, uniform surface on both sides, which is essential for high-end modular furniture and kitchen shutters to prevent gaps or warping.
